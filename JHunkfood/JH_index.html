New York and Chicago make great pizza, but do we really need to truck them into the Carolinas? With a hundred feet of frozen food aisle dedicated to "pretty quick" dinners, I think there's space for the Localvore's "Go-To" name in frozen pizza:

The Jealthy Hunkfood Company!

The Carolinas are one of the few places in the country fortunate enough to have access to not only locally sourced organic wheat, but also a milling craft that's existed since our nation was founded! We have vegetables of every variety, and growers dedicated to keeping the Earth productive for a hundred generations to come. We pride ourselved in adopting new sustainable technologies early. Carolina farmers markets' offer everything under the sun! Carolinians breeze by lush farmland everyday, so why do we still buy an manufactured pizza from who-knows-where? It's time for Jealthy Hunkfood!
